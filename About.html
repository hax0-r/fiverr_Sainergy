About Us

Our Journey

Sainergy was formed in 2001 in San Jose, CA as a software staffing organisation providing highly skilled SAP personnel to large corporations. In 2007, the company decided to expand from pure staffing to implementation of SAP and its related software as a SAP partner for a key customer. The company has now evolved into three key areas of operations - software engineer staffing, simple to complex software implementations and lastly on-site & off-site IT support for existing and completed projects.



At the helm of Sainergy are Priyanka & Dhruv Gupta, who have over 20 years of experience in this industry. Each of Sainergy's practice leads have over 15 years of hands-on SAP experience, whereas the average experience of our consultants in IT is 20+ years and 8+ years in SAP. While building our team, it has always been our vision to ignite the passion for success and excellence in everyone working for Sainergy.
